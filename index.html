Science Lab Safety Rules!!


Follow the Lab Safety Rules or else you will be met face to face with severe consequences, Danger, and possibly Death



Listen to instructions

When you listen to instructions you get to do a cool activity right but when you go off topic...



Don't Run in the Lab

If you run in the lab you might trip and fall and possibly hurt yourself and/or damage the equipment



Call the Teacher Incase of an Emergency

if anyone is injured or an accident happened call a teacher to ensure the safety of all your classmates



Always Wear Safety Goggles

Safety goggles protect your eyes from toxic fumes entering in, without them your eyes are in great danger, much damage could be caused to your eyeballs



Clean your Workspace

cleaning your work space keeps ready for the next class



Wear Proper Attire

wearing the proper attire can prevent your own clothes from any dangers or spills and long hair tied back would prevent any damage to the hair
